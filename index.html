Why Should You Learn AI?

In the context of the 4th industrial revolution, AI has the potential to yield the most significant productivity increase in the history of our civilization. At the same time, this fast change is likely to change the structure of work and leave many people behind.

How it works?

15 sessions of 5h each scheduled on Saturdays so that everyone has an opportunity to learn while building projects powered by AI. Learn from the best open source content such as fast.ai and then focus on implementing social impact and open source projects. The course ends with a DemoDay, in which we celebrate what our fellows have learned as well as featuring their profiles and projects with leading AI Companies.
