How it works?

14 Saturdays of 4 hours each, learning Artificial Intelligence through practical coding exercises and building amazing ML prototypes that make a difference, all in a collaborative way within your cohort and becoming part of the awesome #AIFellows community. Coupled with "how to" conferences, AI theory pills, hackathons and a final Demo Day to showcase your project with Tucson business leaders and hiring managers.

Code 2 Learn

During the first half of the program we learn the foundations of machine-learning and deep-learning by coding exercises on GPU enabled environments in a collaborative way, assisted by facilitators and mentors

Build 2 Learn

In the second half of the program, we build end2end AI powered prototypes using what we learned in the "code2learn" phase, working with the datasets and models of your choice, we address a real social impact problem with AI

Demo Day

Finally, after completing the Saturday sessions, we each deliver a presentation on Demo Day where partners, local business leaders and the AI community celebrates your project and your accomplishments

Be global or local, we appreciate our partners support to help us empower everyone to learn AI
